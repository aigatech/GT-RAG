	The College of Computing is one of the six primary colleges at Georgia Tech.
	The College of Computing houses five schools - the School of Computing Instruction, the School of Computational Science and Engineering, the School of Computer Science, the School of Cybersecurity and Privacy, and the School of Interactive Computing.
	As of Fall 2020, the College of Computing has a total enrollment of 15,245 students, consisting of 3,309 undergrads, 1,377 on-campus MS & PhD students, and 10,559 OMSCS students.[1]
